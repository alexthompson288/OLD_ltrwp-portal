About us

Technology has changed and enriched our lives in so many ways - we think it has a long way to go in education.

Our mission is to make the best quality digital education products and make them affordable and accessible to lots of people.

We have built Learn to read with Pip because reading is the gateway to all knowledge - the most important skill we ever master.

Computers, iPads and other tablets can show rich animations, play music and sound and respond to our touch. They are the most amazing learning tool. We want to make products that take advantage of the potential of these amazing machines to make learning as fun and engaging as we possibly can.

The team

We are a team of educators, teachers, researchers, illustrators, animators, writers, voice artists, sound engineers and software engineers based mainly in London, UK.
